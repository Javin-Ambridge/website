Welcome



Computer Science has always been my passion. Ever since my grade 11 computer science class I knew that this was the field of study I wanted to pursue. I have never faltered in my journey to become a hard working knowledgable programmer and have proactively acquired a wide variety of applicable skills learned through a combination of sources: attending university courses, side projects, self development (online courses), and co-op work terms.

I have delved into many different sectors of the computer science industry. Early on I developed games with the Java library and then expanded that knowledge to work with the LWJGL Java game libraries. I have created numerous heavy algorithmic programs, for example creating a perfect 2 dimensional physics simulator that updates in current time the velocity and angle of up to two balls. I have also programmed many projects for my previous employer, many which are still in use today, more information on these projects can be read on my Resume/Cover letter. Lastly I have also made a Sudoku solver in two languages, one for a side project in Java, and one as a final assignment for my Waterloo class, which was coded in Scheme (Dr. Racket). All of these projects are available in my past projects page (just click here)

My prior job experiences all improved my interpersonal and communication skills which are key in being able to understand the investors goals and being able to work effectively in a team environment. As an English tutor during my 4 year high school term, I learned to think outside the box, communicate effectively, understand another perspective, act quickly and solve various problems with abstract thinking. Dealing effectively with short deadlines meant I had to create a fundamental teaching schedule for each of my students, quickly identify each student's strengths and weaknesses and how to best tutor them to maximize efficiency and results. In addition to being a tutor during the school year, I worked for a total of 3 summers with the City of Toronto, as a Lifeguard and a Wading Pool Attendant liaising with hundreds of clients with differing needs. These experiences, as well as others that are outlined on my resume.
